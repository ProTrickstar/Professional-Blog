What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It enables computers to perform tasks like learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, understanding language, and decision-making — all without direct human intervention.

AI can be broadly classified into:

  • Narrow AI (Weak AI): Highly specialized systems designed for specific tasks (e.g. Siri, Google Search, Face Recognition).
  • General AI (Strong AI): Human-like intelligence capable of learning and performing any task a person can. Still theoretical.

AI technologies evolve through Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, Robotics, Cognitive Computing, and more.
